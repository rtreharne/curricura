What is Curricura?

Curricura ingests your exported Canvas course content and lecture transcripts, embedding them with AI so you can ask questions in natural language and get precise, context-aware answers.

For Students

Quickly locate lecture notes, assessments, and study materials without hunting through menus.

For Instructors

Summarise content, identify gaps, and verify the structure of your course delivery.

For Curriculum Leads

Analyse topic coverage across modules and support curriculum mapping and quality assurance.

How It Works

1. Ingest

Upload a Canvas export and transcript files. Curricura extracts and cleans the content.

2. Embed

We chunk and embed content using OpenAI’s models, storing them in a fast, searchable index.

3. Chat

Ask natural language questions and get accurate, grounded answers from your own materials.

Curricura Roadmap

We are building Curricura step by step to ensure it’s robust, intelligent, and ready for real-world use.

Phase 1 – Canvas & Transcript Ingestion

Upload and parse exported Canvas content and transcript files.

Phase 2 – Embedding & Vector Indexing

Store content in a vector database for fast, semantic search.

Phase 3 – Chat Interface

Natural language Q&A powered by GPT-4 with RAG architecture.

Phase 4 – Contextual Enhancements

Add filtering, transcript navigation, and improved response accuracy.

Phase 5 – Curriculum Planning Tools

Visualise topic distribution and support course audits and QA.

Phase 6 – Admin Tools & Polishing

Internal dashboards, analytics, and workflow optimisations.
